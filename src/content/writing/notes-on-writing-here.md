This is a working notebook — half essay collection, half laboratory log.
I keep it because thinking in public is one of the few tools that reliably
improves my own work. Writing forces the ideas to hold their shape.

## What lives here

Three kinds of material:

- **Writing** — shorter observations, notes on tools, working memos.
- **Research** — longer essays that I intend to hold up over time.
- **Systems** — the software I actually ship, with the reasoning behind it.

The design borrows from field notebooks and chart rooms. Wide margins,
quiet typography, a copper rule where you would otherwise see a marketing
button. Nothing here is trying to sell you anything.

## A small commitment

Every piece is edited. Nothing is generated. Where models helped me think,
I say so. Where I am uncertain, I say so. Where I am wrong, corrections
arrive as amendments rather than silent edits.

> The goal is not to be interesting. The goal is to be *correct*, and
> then, if you can manage it, clear.

That is the whole editorial policy.
