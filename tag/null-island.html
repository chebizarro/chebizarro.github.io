Setting Course for Null Island

Posted on Thu 04 August 2016 in Null Island • Tagged with Open Source, Null Island, Geo • Leave a comment

My wife was recently interviewed for an article in the Wall Street Journal about the fictional location Null Island. We got to talking about how hard would it be to actually charter a boat and go to where the NOAA PIRATA Buoy is moored. If we could do something interesting while we were there we might even be able to crowdfund the money for it. As it turns out, one of my previous jobs was getting people and boats to weird places and doing odd things once they were there. So I set about figuring out what we would need to do.
